15 Minutes of Fame

November 2011

You’ll probably never read this article. You may never even know my name. But, when those fifteen minutes of fame hit, you must be ready. You can bask in its glory and enjoy your peak. Or you can be prepared, and ride that wave. You will do something great one day and people will notice. Many stop right there. Why? You might get an extra 50 Twitter followers that day. Maybe 100. Why not give them a reason to keep coming back? Why not give them a reason to tell their friends to start paying attention to you? There are thousands of intelligent people out there that get lost in the shuffle of this universe we call the Internet. They lose motivation. Why keep building if no one is around to see it? Many engineers have the problem of getting noticed. Not every innovation has that immediate wow factor. The same inability to present one’s self socially can hurt their inventions. Keep ideas around.

Your fifteen minutes will hit…Are you ready?

I am.
